MOVIE RATING GUIDELINES - SPRING 2026
-------------------------------------
G (General): No offensive language, no violence, no drug use.
PG (Parental Guidance): Mild language allowed; brief scary moments.
PG-13 (Caution): Stronger language and intense action sequences.
R (Restricted): Adult themes, strong language, and graphic violence.

COMPLIANCE PROTOCOL:
1. All audience complaints must be reviewed within 48 hours.
2. Flagged scenes must be double-checked by a senior officer.
3. Final approval requires a written justification.
